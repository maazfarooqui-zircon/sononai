MODULE 02 — CERTIQ

THE CERTIFICATION
ROADMAP

From first acoustic prediction to type certificate noise approval — CERTIQ structures every step of the ICAO Annex 16 and EASA SC-VTOL-01 compliance process.

01
SONIQ OUTPUT
ACOUSTIC PREDICTION
Run SONIQ to generate one-third octave SPL spectra at measurement microphone positions for hover, take-off, and flyover profiles.
02
ISO 9613-1
ATMOSPHERIC CORRECTION
Normalise all levels to ICAO reference atmosphere (25°C, 70% RH). Apply frequency-dependent absorption corrections to slant distance.
03
ICAO DOC 9501
PNL + TONE CORRECTION
Calculate Perceived Noise Level from noy tables. Apply tone correction factor C where spectral irregularities exceed 1.5 dB. Compute PNLT = PNL + C.
04
ANNEX 16 APPENDIX 2
EPNL CALCULATION
Integrate PNLT over event duration. EPNdB = PNLT_max + duration correction D. Full uncertainty budget per ICAO requirements.
05
EASA SC-VTOL-01 / ICAO CH.14
COMPLIANCE MARGIN
Compare certified EPNdB against applicable MTOW-dependent limits. Cumulative margin across all measurement points. Report to 0.1 EPNdB.

THE SCIENCE

EVERY MODEL.
EVERY REFERENCE.

No black boxes. Every equation in SONONAI traces to a published, peer-reviewed source.

01
THICKNESS NOISE
Lowson (1970)
J. Acoust. Soc. Am. 47(1B):371–385. Monopole radiation from rotating sources. BPF harmonics via Fourier decomposition.
02
LOADING NOISE
Ffowcs Williams & Hawkings (1969)
Phil. Trans. R. Soc. A 264:321. Dipole radiation from unsteady aerodynamic forces. Dominant at low Mach hover.
03
BROADBAND (BPM)
Brooks, Pope & Marcolini (1989)
NASA RP-1218. Trailing-edge, separation, and tip vortex self-noise. Industry standard broadband model.
04
JCA POROUS MEDIA
Johnson-Champoux-Allard
J. Fluid Mech. 176 + J. Appl. Phys. 70. 5-parameter model with Transfer Matrix Method on rigid backing.
05
MICRO-PERFORATED PANEL
Maa, D.Y. (1998)
J. Acoust. Soc. Am. 104(5):2861. Sub-mm viscous resistance. Aircraft-grade absorber without fibrous fill.
06
EPNL PROCEDURE
ICAO Annex 16 / Doc 9501
Effective Perceived Noise Level. PNL + tone correction C + duration correction D. Uncertainty budget per Appendix 2.
