MODELS & REFERENCES

FOUNDED ON PEER-REVIEWED
AEROACOUSTICS

Every equation in SONONAI traces directly to a published, peer-reviewed source. No black boxes.

01
THICKNESS NOISE
Lowson (1970)
J. Acoust. Soc. Am. 47(1B):371–385
Monopole radiation from rotating sources. BPF harmonics via Fourier decomposition of thickness loading.
02
LOADING NOISE
Ffowcs Williams & Hawkings (1969)
Phil. Trans. R. Soc. A 264:321–342
Dipole radiation from unsteady aerodynamic forces. Dominant at low Mach number in eVTOL hover.
03
BROADBAND (BPM)
Brooks, Pope & Marcolini (1989)
NASA RP-1218
Trailing-edge, separation, and tip vortex self-noise from NACA 0012 blade-element data. Industry standard.
04
JCA POROUS MEDIA
Johnson-Champoux-Allard (1987–1991)
J. Fluid Mech. 176 + J. Appl. Phys. 70
5-parameter semi-phenomenological model. Transfer Matrix Method (TMM) on rigid backing.
05
MICRO-PERFORATED PANEL
Maa, D.Y. (1998)
J. Acoust. Soc. Am. 104(5):2861–2866
Sub-millimetre hole viscous resistance. Aircraft-grade absorber without fibrous fill. Used in cabin liners.
06
CERTIFICATION
ICAO Annex 16 Vol. I (2017)
Amendment 13 + EASA SC-VTOL-01 (2022) + SAE ARP1779
Community noise limits, measurement procedures, EPNdB calculation methodology.
