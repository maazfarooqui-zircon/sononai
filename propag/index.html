Lden ≤ 55 dB (residential zone)
EASA SC-VTOL-01 §F.800 requires Lden ≤ 55 dB at nearest residential façade. Certification basis: CS-36 equivalent for UAM operations. Community noise monitoring plan mandatory above 20 ops/hr.

FAA Order 1050.1F · AC 150/5020-1
—
DNL ≤ 65 dB (compatible land use)
FAA AC 150/5020-1 land use compatibility. DNL >65 dB triggers residential incompatibility. 14 CFR Part 150 noise exposure map required for federally funded vertiports. VTOL ARC interim noise guidance.

WHO Environmental Noise 2018
—
Lnight ≤ 40 dB strong / 55 dB interim
WHO 2018 aircraft Lden 45 dB strong recommendation (interim: 55 dB). Lnight 40 dB recommended. Night curfew 22:00–06:00 strongly advised for all rotor types near residential areas.
Lnight vs 55 dB interim
ICAO Annex 16 Vol. I
—
Cumulative EPNdB margin > 0
Helicopters: ICAO Ch.8. eVTOL: draft Ch.29 / SC-VTOL-01 interim basis. Cumulative margin = limit−measured at all 3 certification points (takeoff, approach, hover). Positive margin required.

UK CAA CAP 1529 · AIC Y 053/2021
—
LAmax ≤ 60 dB at building façade
CAA CAP 1529: 60 dB LAmax at sensitive receptor façade for hover operations. Noise preferential routes required. Route planning approval for residential corridors. Monitoring obligations above 10 ops/hr.
LAmax vs 60 dB limit
